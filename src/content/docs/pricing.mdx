## FAQ

**Is ZeptoDB really free?**
Yes. The full engine is open source under Apache 2.0. No feature gating, no usage limits, no license keys.

**What does Enterprise include?**
Priority support, SLA, deployment assistance, and consulting. The software is the same — you're paying for expertise and guaranteed response times.

**Can I use ZeptoDB in production without Enterprise?**
Absolutely. Many teams run ZeptoDB in production with community support. Enterprise is for teams that need guaranteed SLAs and hands-on help.

**Do you offer managed cloud hosting?**
Not yet. It's on our roadmap. For now, see our [Production Deployment](/deployment/production_deployment/) and [Kubernetes Operations](/operations/kubernetes_operations/) guides.
