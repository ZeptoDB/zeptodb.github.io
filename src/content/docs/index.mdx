## Wherever microseconds matter

Sensor fusion in a humanoid. Closed-loop control on a factory floor. Tick-by-tick risk on a trading desk. Grid telemetry across a region. The workloads look different — the bottleneck is the same: **time-series data arriving faster than a conventional database can ingest, join, and hand to your model.**

ZeptoDB is the microsecond time-series substrate built for that bottleneck. One engine, standard SQL, lock-free ingestion, LLVM JIT execution, and a zero-copy path straight into NumPy and PyTorch.

## Why teams pick ZeptoDB

- **One engine, every workload.** Ingestion, real-time query, historical replay, and ML feature serving in a single binary. No Lambda-architecture tax.
- **Standard SQL, not a DSL.** Your analysts, quants, and ML engineers write SQL with temporal extensions (ASOF JOIN, xbar, EMA). No proprietary language to learn.
- **Zero-copy to the model.** 522ns from column store to NumPy / PyTorch. The difference between a feature store that keeps up and one that doesn't.
- **Edge to cloud.** ARM Graviton edge nodes to multi-node EKS clusters. Parquet HDB on S3 / GCS / NFS for long-tail history.
- **Commercially friendly.** Community edition is free and production-ready on a single node. Enterprise adds clustering, SSO, and audited feature gating — no phone-home.
