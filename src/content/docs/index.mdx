## Why ZeptoDB?

| | **kdb+** | **ClickHouse** | **InfluxDB** | **ZeptoDB** |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Latency** | μs | ms | ms | **μs** |
| **Ingestion** | ~5M/sec | 100K/sec | 50K/sec | **5.52M/sec** |
| **SQL** | ✗ (q lang) | ✓ | InfluxQL | **✓ Standard SQL** |
| **Python** | IPC | — | — | **522ns zero-copy** |
| **ASOF JOIN** | ✓ | ✗ | ✗ | **✓** |
| **License** | $100K+/yr | OSS | OSS | **OSS** |
