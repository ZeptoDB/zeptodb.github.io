The public path for ZeptoDB is GitHub-first: source, issues, discussions, and community feedback.

## Community

- **GitHub Issues** — Bug reports and feature requests: [github.com/zeptodb/zeptodb/issues](https://github.com/zeptodb/zeptodb/issues)
- **GitHub Discussions** — Questions and community help: [github.com/zeptodb/zeptodb/discussions](https://github.com/zeptodb/zeptodb/discussions)
- **Source repository** — Code, releases, and development activity: [github.com/zeptodb/zeptodb](https://github.com/zeptodb/zeptodb)
- **Discord** — Lightweight community chat: [discord.gg/PAtzvCa7](https://discord.gg/PAtzvCa7)

## What to Use

- Use **Issues** for bugs, missing docs, and specific feature requests.
- Use **Discussions** for architecture questions, roadmap feedback, and examples.
- Use the **Quick Start** when you want the shortest runnable path: [Getting Started →](/getting-started/quick_start/)
